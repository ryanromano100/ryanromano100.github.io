Suddenly found yourself homeless? Print this page.

  • Step 1: Do not panic. Take a moment to calm yourself.

  • Step 2: If you are in a dysfunctional family, you might be interested in safeguarding money stored in your bank account. If you have a "joint account" or are not sure, go to a bank and open a new account (you will need a state issued ID) and move your money because it's not considered "yours" alone.

  • Step 3: Here are some items you might want to collect. Some of these can be useful for finding a job.

    • Social Security Card
    • Birth Certificate
    • Vaccination records
    • Passport
    • Cell Phone
    • Cell phone plan
    • Pen and paper

  • Step 4: Call family, or call friends, or call a homeless shelter from Google Maps, or call 211 (not every state) which is a social service number.
